Dimension is an AI assistant that connects to the tools you already use — Gmail, Slack, Linear, Notion, GitHub, Google Calendar, and more — to handle tasks, surface what matters, and keep you on top of your work.

<Note>Dimension is available on web, Mac, Windows, and iOS. [Get started in under 5 minutes →](/quickstart)</Note>

## What Dimension does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Chat" icon="message-bot" href="/chat">
    Chat with Dimension to take actions across your tools — send emails, create issues, schedule meetings, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workflows" icon="arrow-progress" href="/workflows">
    Automate recurring tasks with scheduled or event-triggered workflows that run in the background.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skills" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/skills">
    Teach Dimension how to handle specific tasks your way, and it gets better over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Artifacts" icon="file-lines" href="/artifacts">
    Generate presentations, spreadsheets, documents, and PDFs directly from chat.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
