Dimension connects to your most sensitive tools — your email, calendar, code, and documents. We take that responsibility seriously. Security isn't an afterthought — it's built into every layer of how we handle your data.

<Note>Dimension never trains AI models on your data, never sells your information, and never shares it with third parties for advertising. Your data is yours.</Note>

## Encryption

All data is protected with enterprise-grade encryption in transit and at rest. OAuth tokens are stored securely and are not accessible to Dimension personnel.

## Your controls

You're always in control of your data:

- **Disconnect an integration** and Dimension permanently and completely deletes all associated data
- **Delete your account** from Settings to permanently remove all your data, integrations, and conversations
- **Revoke access** to any third-party integration through the provider's settings at any time

## Breach notification

In the event of a data breach, Dimension will notify affected users within 72 hours.
