Dimension doesn't just wait for you to ask — it works in the background across your connected tools to surface what matters, draft responses, and prepare you for your day.

<Note>You can toggle each proactive feature on or off individually in **Settings > Preference > Background Agents**.</Note>

## Morning Briefing

Every morning, Dimension prepares a personalized briefing with:

- **Weather** for your location
- **Your schedule** — meetings, deep work windows, and personal plans at a glance
- **What needs your attention** across email, Slack, and calendar

The briefing appears on your home screen when you open Dimension.

## Catch Up

Dimension summarizes what you missed across three tabs:

- **Email** — Unread emails with AI-generated thread summaries and draft replies ready to send
- **Slack** — Unread messages with thread summaries and suggested responses
- **Meetings** — Upcoming meetings with attendee details and prep notes

Each item comes with a pre-drafted reply that you can edit and send directly.

## AI Inbox

Dimension automatically manages your email in the background:

- **Auto-tagging** — Emails are categorized and labeled automatically
- **Auto-drafting** — Responses are drafted in your tone, ready for you to review and send

## Meeting Prep

Before each meeting, Dimension pulls context from your connected tools and generates prep notes — what the meeting is about, relevant documents, and key context from previous conversations. You can share prep notes with attendees directly.

## Action Items

Dimension scans your email, Slack, and calendar for urgent items and notifies you so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Evening Recap

A summary of your day — what happened, what got done, and what's left.

## Todos

Dimension suggests action items by scanning your email, Slack, calendar, Linear, and GitHub. You can:

- **Add** a suggestion as a todo
- **Dismiss** suggestions you don't need
- **Assign a todo to Dimension** — it creates a background thread and works through the task autonomously, notifying you when it's done or if it needs your input

## Managing these features

You can turn each background agent on or off individually in **Settings > Preference > Background Agents**. The available toggles are:

- Action Items
- Evening Recap
- Morning Briefing
- Email Tagging
- Email Auto-Drafting
- Meeting Prep
