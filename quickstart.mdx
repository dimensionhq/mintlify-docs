<Steps>

<Step title="Create your account">
Go to [dimension.dev](https://dimension.dev) and sign up with Google or SSO.
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Google Workspace">
The first thing Dimension asks you to do is connect Google Workspace. Most core features depend on it:

- **Dimension in chat** can read and send emails, manage your calendar, search your Drive, and edit Docs and Sheets
- **Morning Briefing** pulls from your calendar and email
- **AI Inbox** auto-tags and drafts replies from Gmail
- **Meeting Prep** uses your calendar events and related docs

<Tip>Connecting Google unlocks Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Slides — all in one step.</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect your other tools">
You'll then have the option to connect tools like Slack, Linear, Notion, GitHub, Vercel, and more. You can skip this and connect them later from the **Integrations** page.
</Step>

<Step title="Get the apps">
Dimension is available on the web today, with native apps on the way.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web">
    Head to [dimension.dev](https://dimension.dev) — no install needed.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mac & Windows">
    *Coming soon.* The desktop app for Mac and Windows is currently in development.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS">
    *Coming soon.* The iOS app will be available on the App Store soon.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
</Step>

<Step title="Start chatting">
Send your first message. Try something like:

- "What's on my calendar today?"
- "Summarize my unread emails"
- "Create a Linear issue for the login bug we discussed in Slack"
- "Draft a presentation about our Q4 results"
</Step>

</Steps>
